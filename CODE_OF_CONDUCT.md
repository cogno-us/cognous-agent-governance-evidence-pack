# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and productive experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment of any kind
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct, or ban contributors whose behavior is harmful.
